Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Manaaki Care ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of the individuals whose data you manage using our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our client/case management software.

We take the protection of personal data very seriously, especially given the sensitive nature of the information that may be processed through our platform in the context of social and community services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information for different purposes:

2.1 Account Information

When you register for Manaaki Care, we collect information such as your name, email address, organization name, and contact details.

2.2 Client Data

As a case management platform, our service is designed to store and process information about your clients. This may include personal and sensitive information such as names, contact details, case notes, assessments, service plans, and outcome measurements. You are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from your clients for the collection and processing of their data.

2.3 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use our platform, including log data, device information, and analytics data. This helps us improve our services and ensure security.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:

  • Providing and maintaining our service
  • Notifying you about changes to our service
  • Providing customer support
  • Gathering analysis or valuable information to improve our service
  • Monitoring the usage of our service
  • Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information and client data.

Our security measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular backups and disaster recovery procedures
  • Staff training on data protection and security

5. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information and the client data you manage through our platform only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your information to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • The right to access, update, or delete your information
  • The right to rectification
  • The right to object to processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent

As a data processor for your clients' information, we will assist you in fulfilling data subject requests related to data processed through our platform.

7. Third-Party Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our service, provide the service on our behalf, perform service-related services, or assist us in analyzing how our service is used. These third parties have access to your personal information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: privacy@manaakicare.com
  • By visiting the contact page on our website
