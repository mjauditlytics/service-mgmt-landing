Key Features

Everything you need to manage cases effectively and support whānau.

Comprehensive Care Plans

Create detailed, culturally responsive care plans that address the holistic needs of whānau. Our structured approach ensures nothing is overlooked while maintaining flexibility for individual needs.

  • Customizable templates
  • Goal tracking and progress monitoring
  • Collaborative planning tools

Holistic Wellbeing Assessment

Our assessment framework is built on Te Whare Tapa Whā and other culturally responsive models, ensuring a comprehensive view of wellbeing across all domains.

  • Culturally responsive assessment tools
  • Strengths-based approach
  • Visual progress tracking

Outcome Measurement

Track and report on outcomes that matter, with customizable dashboards and reporting tools that help demonstrate impact and fulfill funding requirements.

  • Customizable reporting dashboards
  • Export options for funding reports
  • Trend analysis and insights
